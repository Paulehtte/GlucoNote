❓ Help Center

How do I log a glucose reading?

Tap the ＋ button at the bottom. Use the keypad, pick your mealtime, then tap ✓ to save.

What is the comfortable glucose range?

For most adults, 70–180 mg/dL is considered comfortable. Always follow your doctor's personal target for you.

How do I select a date range for reports?

On the Reports screen, tap one of the range buttons (7 Days, 14 Days, 30 Days, or All Time). A custom date range option is also available.

How do I export my data?

Go to Reports → tap Export as PDF, Export as CSV, or Email to Doctor. The PDF is professionally formatted with a cover section and full data table.

Is my data private?

Yes — all data stays only on your device in your browser's local storage. Nothing is sent to any server.

What is GlucoBuddy?

GlucoBuddy is your built-in chat companion offering gentle advice about food, motivation, and managing readings.
